Overview

WhyGo language center does not only focus on test preparation such as TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC & SAT but also on business English and daily life English speaking, listening, reading courses. Additionally, WhyGo offers both one-on-one classes as well as small and large group classes. Even though one-on-one teaching is the most effective way to improve your English language skills in a short period of time, group classes are more interested for those who are not in a rush.

Test Preparation

The test preparation or exam preparation courses are a form of English learning that prepares students for admission to institutions of higher education and international studies.

Courses

IELTS

The IELTS qualification is an entry requirement for universities and further education in the UK, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand. Many governments and international employers also recognize it.

TOEFL

The TOEFL Qualification is the most popular exam for North American English, and the preferred English language qualification and entry requirement for universities in the USA and Canada.

TOEIC

TOEIC, is an internationally recognized English language examination designed to test the everyday language skills of people who use English in their workplace.

SAT

The SAT is a globally recognized college admission test that lets you show colleges what you know and how well you can apply that knowledge.
