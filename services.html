Test Preparation

It is our core business to help prepare students for English tests such as TOEFL, IELTS and TOEIC, this includes basic grammar classes for those who need to start from the beginning. WhyGo specifically focuses on training young adults that want to improve their English for either an English examination or general language improvement.

Our TOEFL, IELST & TOEIC courses start every second month and are divided into 3 categories; beginner, intermediate and advanced. Every course takes about 2 months after which a level test will be given to check the student’s performance.

Study Abroad

WhyGo is an expert for Study Abroad: The WhyGO consulting and application services cover all aspects of studying abroad. Get information on study programs, summer schools, higher education or language education abroad. Having a consultation with one of our international experts in free of charge for all students and professionals from Taiwan. We are specialized in advising students who want to study in North America, United Kingdom and the European Union.

We offer 3 different kinds of packages for those who those who need help for either applying for an international university, improving English capabilities, writing application letters and portfolio’s and help prepare for required language tests such as TOEFL, IELTS or TOEIC.
