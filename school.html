About WhyGo Language Center

Studying English with a foreign trainer not only ensures learning correct pronunciation and sentence structure (syntax), but also provides valuable tips on how to adapt your communications and behaviors to the North American culture. This is particularly helpful for those wishing to do business with large corporations either in Taiwan or in a foreign country or for students wishing to study abroad.

Why study at WhyGo?

Our unique schools offers supreme quality and highly effective English courses that will help you reach your goals!

Our Trainers

One of WhyGo's outstanding features is the exceptional quality of its teaching staff. The center was founded by Canadian and American teachers with years of experience and highly specialized skills who are keen on providing a superior quality of education. Its trainers include authors who have published numerous books on teaching Business English. WhyGo trainers create and make use of their own teaching material. A contributing factor to the school’s distinctive curriculum. WhyGo also provides publishing and editing services for outside sources.

Our Location

Our small school is nested between the two big SOGO stores on the third floor of ZhongXiao East Road, section 4 and number 2. The school is uniquely located in the heart of Taipei City, easy accessible by MRT, Bus and U-Bike. Our school is equipped with a state of the art computer study center, coffee bar and several classrooms. We offer a wonderful atmosphere to study English!

Our Courses

WhyGo language center does not only focus on test preparation such as TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC & SAT but also on business English and daily life English speaking, listening, reading courses. Additionally, WhyGo offers both one-on-one classes as well as small and large group classes. Even though one-on-one teaching is the most effective way to improve your English language skills in a short period of time, group classes are more interested for those who are not in a rush.

Our Customers

We are and have been providing business English courses to a variety of valued clients. WhyGo is one of the few language institutes in Taipei that offers group trainings on the clients’ location. We’re proud to mention the following companies that have used our survives.
