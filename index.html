The WhyGo Language Center

WhyGo Language center is a unique school offering a variety of services that are specially designed to reach the goals of business people and students that want to go abroad. Our personalized services and outstanding study center sets WhyGo apart from other language schools in Taiwan as one of the best places to improve your English. Not only are all our teachers highly experienced native English teachers, our school and study center have 100% English speaking environment that will help to improve your communications skills significantly.

Additionally, our school is located in the heart of Taipei City, right between the two Sogo’s and ZhongXiao Fuxing. Despite the highly central located location, the school has a great atmosphere and is the perfect place to study in quietness and peace. Our teachers are highly experienced and undergo several grammar and vocabulary tests before they are allowed to teach at our language center. Yet, we offer a variety of teachers that ranges from highly senior teachers to less expensive but more junior teachers.

Testimonials

Please read what some of our students have to say about studying at WhyGo Language Institute in Taipei.

Angela TOEFL 98

Hello, my name is Angela; I got a 98 on my TOEFL test on 23 January 2010. I started classes with Steven at the WhyGo language center about 4 months ago and I came here about twice a week.

I think I really learned a lot from Steven who is a wonderful teacher. He pointed out my bad writing habits after reading only one or maybe two of my writing tasks and then we worked together to get rid of these bad writing habits.

After plenty of practice, my writing skills progressed so much and allowed me to score 25 (for writing) in my TOEFL test. I felt like a dream comes true, a grade that I can’t ever believe that I’ve got it, and I attribute this high score to Steven’s teaching. Also he uses a creative way to help me improve my speaking skills. We always to a lot if interesting practices, which not only push me to think and speak English at the same time without hesitation but also help me defeat the fear towards speaking in TOEFL test.

Zoe IELTS 7.0

When I decided to prepare for the IELTS test, I went a business cram school with a very famous teacher. However there were always more than 30 students in one classroom and the teacher did not have enough time to answers everyone’s questions. After having studied for 6 months I just got a 5.5 for my IELTS test, which really frustrated me.

However, I come to WhyGo a few months later where I only stayed for 2 months. The atmosphere in the school was warm and fragrant; moreover, they allowed students to study there all day that makes it different from other language schools. The teachers had more than enough time to answer my questions and my English improved rapidly. Within 2 months I got a 7.0 for my IELTS test and had a great English learning experience.
