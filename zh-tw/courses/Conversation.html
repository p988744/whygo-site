About Conversation

Most Taiwanese are afriad of making mistakes so they draw the sentence pattern in their mind before they speak. However, language is a tool for communication, and the key point is to communicate with people. That's why we focus on communication and fluency rather than correctness. All of our students can carry on an everyday conversation without much difficulty, and we also teach them strategies for an academic conversation.
