The School

The Whygo Language Center.

WhyGo Language is a unique school offering a variety of services that are specially designed to reach the goals of business people and students that want to go abroad. Our personalized services and outstanding study center sets WhyGo apart from other language schools in Taiwan and is one of the best places to improve your English. Our Courses

WhyGo offers a variety of courses so that there is a suitable program for everybody that would like to improve their English.

WhyGo offers a variety of courses so that there is a suitable program for everybody who would like to improve their English. We do not only focus on test preparation such as TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC & GMAT but also on business English and general daily life speaking, listing, writing and reading classes. Additionally, we both offer one on one classes as well as small and large group classes. Even though 1:1 teaching is a very effective way to develop language skills in a short period of time, group classes are more interested for those who are not in a rush.
